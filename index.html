About Me

Building the next generation of leaders

I'm the Founder & CEO of MXC Ignite LLP — a startup architect who builds ecosystems, drives innovation programs, and shapes the next generation of leaders. My work sits at the intersection of entrepreneurship, strategic communication, and community building.

I founded MXC Ignite to solve a real problem: India's most talented people are invisible. They lack exposure, mentorship, and the right ecosystems. MXC exists to fix that — creating a space where grassroots talent becomes global leaders.
